Questions about Wellness Harmony

Quick answers on classes, schedules, and app integrations—tailored for gentle, low‑impact fitness.

  • Begin with our Foundations classes (yoga or pilates). They focus on breath, alignment, and gentle strength, perfect for adults easing into movement.

  • Most flows run 10–30 minutes. For steady progress, aim for 2–4 sessions a week with at least one restorative day.

  • A mat is ideal. Optional props—like blocks or a strap—help with comfort and alignment. We offer prop‑free modifications in every class.

  • Yes. All sessions are available in‑studio and live online. You’ll receive a secure link after booking—camera optional, guidance included.

  • Connect your account in settings to log session minutes and mindful minutes. Your streaks and activity history update automatically.

  • Cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before class and your credit returns automatically. Running late? Message us—we’ll help.
